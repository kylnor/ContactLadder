## MODULE AN2: ACTIVITY ANALYTICS

### Tracking Your Outreach & Engagement

**WHY (Video - 3-4 min)**

**Hook:** "Ninja Selling says relationships drive business. Activity Analytics proves whether you're actually building those relationships or just thinking about it."

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### Activity Metrics Explained

**Outreach = YOU initiated contact:**
- Emails you sent (first contact in a thread)
- Calls you made (outbound)
- Texts you sent (first in conversation)
- Notes you added

**Reactions = THEY responded:**
- Emails they replied to
- Calls they returned
- Texts they sent back
- Meetings they accepted

**Key Ratios:**

| Ratio | What It Measures | Healthy Range | What It Means |
|-------|------------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Outreach:Reaction** | How many contacts to get 1 response | 3:1 to 5:1 | Lower = better engagement |
| **Email Open Rate** | % of emails opened | 25-40% | Higher = better subject lines/timing |
| **Response Rate** | % of outreaches that get responses | 20-35% | Higher = stronger relationships |

### What Cloze Activity Analytics Shows

**Daily/Weekly/Monthly Views:**
- Total outreaches
- Total reactions
- Outreach/reaction ratio
- Trends over time (getting better or worse?)

**By Contact Type:**
- Outreach to Advocates vs. Network
- Response rates by Segment
- Identify which segments need more attention

**By Channel:**
- Email outreach vs. reactions
- Phone outreach vs. reactions
- Text outreach vs. reactions
- Which channel gets best response?

   - Outreach count

- Mass emailing without engagement (spray and pray)
- Email quality likely poor (templates not personalized)
- Phone/text work great - do more of those

### Part 3: Set Activity Goals

1. **Go to Agenda ‚Üí Goals** (if using Goals feature)
2. **Set weekly targets:**
   - Outreach goal: 50/week
   - Reaction goal: 15/week
3. **Track progress daily**
4. **Adjust based on analytics**

**Using Goals + Analytics Together:**

- **Goals** = forward-looking targets
- **Analytics** = backward-looking results
- **Monthly:** Compare goals vs. actual, adjust next month's goals

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "My outreach is high but reactions are low. What's wrong?"**
- A: Quality over quantity. You're reaching out to too many people who don't know/remember you, or your messages aren't compelling. Focus on fewer, warmer contacts with personalized outreach.

**Q: "How do I improve my email open rate?"**
- A: Better subject lines (personalized, specific), send at optimal times (9-11am or 1-3pm), clean your list (remove non-responders), segment better (right message to right audience).

**Q: "What's a 'good' outreach number per week?"**
- A: Depends on your business. Ninja Selling suggests 25-50 meaningful outreaches/week. Track what converts for YOU, optimize from there.

**Q: "Does Cloze count automated campaign emails as outreach?"**
- A: Yes, if they're sent from your account. This can skew numbers if you have large drip campaigns running.

### Understanding Your Deal Flow

**WHY (Video - 3-4 min)**

**Hook:** "How many deals are in your pipeline right now? How many are Hot vs. Warm? How long do they stay in each stage? Pipeline Analytics answers this instantly."

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### Pipeline Metrics Explained

**Snapshot Metrics (Point in Time):**
- Total active buyers (Warm + Hot)
- Total active sellers (Warm + Hot)
- Total in contract
- Distribution across stages

**Flow Metrics (Over Time):**
- New leads added this month
- Leads that moved Warm ‚Üí Hot
- Leads that moved Hot ‚Üí In Contract

- Deals lost

**Time Metrics:**
- Average time in each stage
- Time from first contact to close
- Bottlenecks (stages where deals stall)

```

- Warm: 24

- Warm: 12

**Movement This Month:**

```
Buyers:
- New leads: 15
- Warm ‚Üí Hot: 5
- Hot ‚Üí Contract: 2
- Closed: 1
- Lost: 3

Conversion Rates:
- Warm ‚Üí Hot: 21% (5 of 24)
- Hot ‚Üí Contract: 25% (2 of 8)

```

**HOW (Video - 4-5 min)**

*Screen recording showing:*

### Part 1: View Pipeline Overview

1. **Go to Analytics ‚Üí Pipeline**
2. **View current snapshot:**
   - Deals by Stage (chart/graph)
   - Total pipeline value (if using Properties with values)
3. **Select time period** for trends:
   - This month
   - This quarter
   - YTD

### Part 2: Analyze Stage Distribution

**Healthy Pipeline Distribution:**

```
Warm: 60-70% of pipeline (building relationships)
Hot: 20-30% of pipeline (active negotiations)
In Contract: 5-10% of pipeline (closing deals)
```

**Problem Distributions:**

**Too Many Warm, Few Hot:**
```
Warm: 95%
Hot: 4%
Contract: 1%
```
- **Problem:** Not moving people through pipeline
- **Fix:** More aggressive follow-up, better qualifying

**Too Many Hot, Nothing Closing:**
```
Warm: 20%
Hot: 70%
Contract: 10%
```
- **Problem:** Hot buyers not finding properties, or you're marking people Hot prematurely
- **Fix:** Better property matching, realistic Hot classification

### Part 3: Identify Bottlenecks

**Scenario:** Deals stall at Hot stage for 90+ days

**Analysis:**
1. **Filter:** Stage = Hot + Added >90 days ago
2. **Export list**
3. **Review individually:**
   - What's blocking them?
   - Unrealistic expectations?
   - Waiting for external factor?
4. **Action:** Re-qualify or move to Lost

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "What's a 'good' pipeline size?"**
- A: Depends on transaction goals. Rule of thumb: 3x your monthly target in Warm/Hot combined. If you want 2 closings/month, maintain 6+ active Hot buyers.

**Q: "How long should someone stay in Warm?"**
- A: 30-90 days typically. Beyond that, either move to Hot (if progressing) or Lost (if not).

**Q: "What if my pipeline is empty?"**
- A: Lead generation problem. Focus on: sphere outreach, open houses, online leads, referral requests. Can't work an empty pipeline.

### Where Leads Drop Off

**WHY (Video - 3-4 min)**

**Hook:** "100 leads come in. 10 close. What happened to the other 90? Conversion Analytics shows you exactly where they fall out."

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### The Conversion Funnel

**Typical Real Estate Funnel:**

```
Lead (SOI/New Contact)
    ‚Üì (Classify & Qualify)
Warm Buyer/Seller
    ‚Üì (Nurture & Educate)
Hot Buyer/Seller
    ‚Üì (Active Showings/Listing)

    ‚Üì (Navigate Inspection/Financing)

```

**Conversion Metrics to Track:**

| Stage Transition | Healthy Rate | Red Flag Rate | What It Measures |
|------------------|--------------|---------------|------------------|
| **Lead ‚Üí Warm** | 30-50% | <20% | Lead quality & initial qualification |
| **Warm ‚Üí Hot** | 15-25% | <10% | Nurturing effectiveness |
| **Hot ‚Üí Contract** | 30-50% | <20% | Showing/listing effectiveness |
| **Contract ‚Üí Closed** | 75-90% | <60% | Transaction management |

**Example Conversion Report:**

```
Q4 2024 Conversions (Buyers):

100 New Leads
    ‚Üì 42% converted
42 Warm Buyers
    ‚Üì 19% converted
8 Hot Buyers
    ‚Üì 50% converted

    ‚Üì 75% closed
3 Closed Deals

Overall Lead ‚Üí Close: 3% ‚ö†Ô∏è
```

**Analysis:**
- Lead ‚Üí Warm is good (42%)
- **Warm ‚Üí Hot is weak (19%)** ‚¨ÖÔ∏è BOTTLENECK
- Hot ‚Üí Contract is solid (50%)
- Contract ‚Üí Close is good (75%)

**Action:** Focus on Warm nurturing - improve follow-up, property matching, relationship building

### Calculating Your Conversion Rates

**Manual Method (if Cloze doesn't auto-calculate):**

1. **Export contacts by Stage**
2. **Count transitions over time period:**
   - How many started as Warm on Jan 1?
   - How many moved to Hot by Jan 31?
   - Conversion rate = (Hot / Warm) √ó 100
3. **Track monthly**
4. **Identify trends**

**HOW (Video - 5-6 min)**

*Screen recording showing:*

### Part 1: Access Conversion Analytics

1. **Go to Analytics ‚Üí Conversions** (if available)
2. **Select funnel:**
   - Buyer funnel
   - Seller funnel
   - Combined
3. **Select time period:**
   - Last month
   - Last quarter
   - YTD

   - Stage-to-stage percentages
   - Visual funnel diagram

### Part 2: Identify Problem Areas

**Scenario:** Low Warm ‚Üí Hot conversion

**Steps to Diagnose:**

1. **Filter:** Warm buyers added 90+ days ago, still Warm
2. **Export list**
3. **Review patterns:**
   - Are you following up regularly? (Check timeline)
   - Are they responding? (Check last contact date)
   - Do you know their criteria? (Check custom fields)
4. **Common issues:**
   - Infrequent follow-up (fix: set reminders)
   - Not sending listings (fix: proactive property sending)
   - Mis-classified as Warm when they're cold (fix: move to Lost)

### Part 3: Improve Conversion Rates

**Tactic 1: Shorten Time in Stage**

- **Track:** Average days in Warm stage
- **Goal:** Reduce from 90 days to 60 days
- **How:** More frequent touchpoints, better qualifying

**Tactic 2: Re-Qualify Regularly**

- **Every 30 days:** Review all Warm contacts
- **Ask:** Still looking? Timeline changed? Ready to move to Hot?
- **Action:** Hot, Lost, or stay Warm with updated timeline

**Tactic 3: Automate Nurturing**

- **Use Campaigns:** Drip content to Warm buyers/sellers
- **Keeps them engaged** without manual outreach
- **Moves them toward Hot** with educational content

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "What if my Lead ‚Üí Warm rate is low (<20%)?"**
- A: Lead quality problem. Your lead sources are sending unqualified/uninterested people. Either improve qualification questions or change lead sources.

**Q: "What if my Hot ‚Üí Contract rate is low (<20%)?"**
- A: Either your Hot criteria is too loose (you're marking people Hot prematurely), or your property matching/showing skills need work.

**Q: "How do I know if my conversion rates are good?"**
- A: Compare to your own historical data (improving or declining?), then benchmark against industry averages. But YOUR trend matters more than industry comparison.

## MODULE AN5: LEAD SOURCE & ROI ANALYTICS

### Know What Marketing Works

**WHY (Video - 3-4 min)**

**Hook:** "You spend $10K/month on marketing. Which $5K should you kill and which $5K should you double? Lead source analytics tells you."

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### Tracking Lead Sources

**Required Setup:**
- **Custom Field:** "Lead Source" (Dropdown)
- **Options:** Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Referral - Past Client, Referral - Agent, Open House, SOI, Yard Sign, Walk-In, Other

**Every new lead ‚Üí assign Lead Source immediately**

### ROI Calculation

**Formula:**

```
ROI = (Gross Commission from Source - Cost of Source) / Cost of Source √ó 100%
```

**Example:**

**Zillow:**
- Monthly cost: $3,000
- Annual cost: $36,000
- Leads: 120/year
- Closed: 4
- Avg commission: $9,000
- Gross commission: $36,000
- **ROI:** ($36,000 - $36,000) / $36,000 = 0% (break even)

**Facebook Ads:**
- Monthly cost: $1,500
- Annual cost: $18,000
- Leads: 80/year
- Closed: 8
- Avg commission: $9,000
- Gross commission: $72,000
- **ROI:** ($72,000 - $18,000) / $18,000 = **300%** üî•

**Action:** Kill Zillow (or reduce spend), double Facebook

### Lead Source Report Structure

| Lead Source | Leads | Warm | Hot | Closed | Conv % | Cost | Comm | ROI |
|-------------|-------|------|-----|--------|--------|------|------|-----|
| Zillow | 120 | 45 | 10 | 4 | 3.3% | $36K | $36K | 0% |
| Facebook | 80 | 35 | 12 | 8 | 10% | $18K | $72K | 300% |
| Referral - PC | 30 | 25 | 15 | 10 | 33% | $0 | $90K | ‚àû |
| Open House | 50 | 15 | 5 | 2 | 4% | $2K | $18K | 800% |
| SOI | 40 | 30 | 10 | 5 | 12.5% | $0 | $45K | ‚àû |

**Insights:**
- **Best ROI:** Past client referrals, SOI (free sources)
- **Best paid ROI:** Facebook, Open Houses
- **Worst ROI:** Zillow (break-even)
- **Action:** Invest more in referral programs, open houses, Facebook

### Measuring Email Effectiveness

**WHY (Video - 2-3 min)**

**Hook:** "You send 100 emails. 30 people open them. 5 people click links. 1 person responds. Email analytics shows you how to get that to 60 opens, 20 clicks, 10 responses."

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### Email Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Healthy Benchmark | Red Flag |
|--------|------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Open Rate** | % of recipients who opened | 25-40% | <15% |
| **Click Rate** | % who clicked a link | 10-20% | <5% |
| **Response Rate** | % who replied | 15-30% | <10% |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | % who opted out | <1% | >5% |

**Campaign Analytics:**

For drip campaigns and automated sequences:
- **Enrollment rate:** How many contacts enter campaign
- **Completion rate:** How many finish the sequence
- **Drop-off points:** Which email causes most unsubscribes
- **Conversion rate:** How many take desired action

**Example Email Campaign Report:**

```

Email 4: Open 45%, Click 15%, Drop 8% ‚ö†Ô∏è

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "My open rates are terrible (<15%). What's wrong?"**
- A: Poor subject lines (too generic, spammy), bad send timing (weekends, late night), or your list has too many cold contacts who don't remember you.

**Q: "People open but don't click or respond. Why?"**
- A: Email content doesn't compel action. Add clearer CTAs, make links more obvious, ask direct questions that require responses.

**Q: "How do I reduce unsubscribes?"**
- A: Send less frequently, segment better (right message to right audience), make content valuable (not just "checking in"), give options (pause emails without unsubscribing).
