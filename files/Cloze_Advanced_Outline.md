**WHAT IF (Text)**

- Q: "What if I want to see the same person in two Audiences?"

- Q: "What if I create too many Audiences?"

- Q: "Can I share Audiences with my team?"

- Q: "What's the difference between an Audience and a Tag search?"

### Personal Emails at Scale

**WHY (Video - 2-3 min)**

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

**What's Mail Merge?**

Mail Merge sends the same email to multiple contacts, but each one arrives as a personal email from your inboxâ€”not a marketing blast. Recipients see it as a one-on-one message from you.

**Mail Merge vs. ActivePipe/Marketing Emails:**

| Feature | Mail Merge | ActivePipe/Marketing |
|---------|------------|----------------------|
| Looks like | Personal email from you | Newsletter/marketing email |
| Sent from | Your actual inbox | Marketing platform |
| Best for | <500 people, relationship-based | 500+ people, branded content |
| Reply goes to | Your inbox directly | Your inbox (varies) |
| Open tracking | Yes | Yes |
| Personalization | Name, basic fields | More design options |

**When to Use Mail Merge:**

- New listing announcement to interested buyers
- Event invitation to advocates/fans
- Market update to a specific area (tag-based)
- "Haven't talked in a while" re-engagement
- Holiday or birthday greetings (if not automated)

**When NOT to Use Mail Merge:**

- More than ~500 recipients (use ActivePipe)
- Highly designed/branded content (use ActivePipe)
- Automated drip sequences (that's Campaignsâ€”see Deep Dive)

**HOW (Video - 3-4 min)**

*Screen recording showing:*

1. Start with a filtered list or Audience (example: #lakelasvegas tag)
2. Select all contacts (checkbox at top)
3. Click the checkmark icon at bottom
4. Select "Mail Merge"
5. Write the email subject and body
6. Show the personalization options (first name, etc.)
7. **Show the AI tweak option** - "You can use the lightbulb to adjust tone or rewrite"
8. Preview what recipients will see
9. Hit Send
10. Show where to find open/click tracking after sending

**Key Point:**

> "Each email is individual. If Sarah replies, it comes to your inbox like any normal email. She has no idea 27 other people got a similar message."

**WHAT IF (Text)**

- Q: "What if I want to customize individual emails before sending?"
  - A: You can! Before hitting send, you can review and edit each email individually. Most people don't, but the option exists.

- Q: "What if someone unsubscribes?"
  - A: Mail Merge doesn't have an unsubscribe link by default (it's personal email). If someone asks to stop, remove their tag or adjust their segment so they don't appear in that Audience.

- Q: "What if I send to too many people and get flagged as spam?"
  - A: This is why we say under 500. Your email provider may throttle you if you send more than that at once. For large lists, use ActivePipe.

- Q: "Can I schedule a Mail Merge for later?"
  - A: Yesâ€”there's a scheduling option when you send. Great for "send at 9am Tuesday" timing.

- Q: "Can I see who opened my email?"
  - A: Yes. Cloze tracks opens and clicks. You'll see this in the contact's timeline and in your Agenda alerts.

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

**What Can You Bulk Edit?**

| Field | Example Use Case |
|-------|------------------|
| Segment | Move 50 new contacts from SOI to Network after an event |
| Stage | Move all your warm buyers to hot after market shift |
| Tags | Add #openhouse2024 to everyone who attended |
| Tags (remove) | Clean up old tags from people who no longer match |

**What You CAN'T Bulk Edit:**

- Contact info (phone, email, address)â€”too risky for mistakes
- Notes, At a Glance, FORD infoâ€”these are personal per contact

**The Bulk Edit Flow:**

1. Filter or View to find your group
2. Select all (or select specific contacts)
3. Click the checkmark icon
4. Choose your action (change segment, add tag, etc.)
5. Confirm
6. Done

**HOW (Video - 2-3 min)**

*Screen recording showing:*

1. Go to People, apply a filter (example: Segment = SOI, Tag = #eventattendee)

**Warning to Include:**

> "Bulk editing is powerfulâ€”which means mistakes are powerful too. Always double-check your filter before you select all. If you accidentally change 500 contacts to the wrong segment, you'll be bulk-editing them back."

**WHAT IF (Text)**

- Q: "What if I select too many people by accident?"
  - A: You can deselect individuals before applying the change. Or undo by bulk-editing them back. There's no permanent damage, just extra work.

- Q: "Can I bulk edit from my phone?"
  - A: Limited functionality on mobile. For major bulk work, use desktop.

- Q: "What if I want to bulk edit AND send an email to the same group?"
  - A: Do the bulk edit first, then do the Mail Merge. Two separate actions, same filtered group.
