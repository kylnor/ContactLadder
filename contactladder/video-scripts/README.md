## Recording Guidelines

### Before Recording

1. **Review the script thoroughly** - Read it 2-3 times
2. **Prepare your demo environment** - Clean test account with sample data
3. **Test your screen recording** - Audio levels, resolution (1920x1080 minimum)
4. **Close unnecessary tabs/apps** - Clean desktop, no notifications

### During Recording

1. **Speak naturally** - Scripts are guides, not word-for-word teleprompters
2. **Pause between sections** - Makes editing easier
3. **Go slow on screen actions** - Let viewers follow along
4. **Use cursor highlights** - Show where you're clicking
5. **Repeat key points** - Emphasize important concepts

### Screen Recording Settings

- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps minimum
- **Audio**: 48kHz, mono is fine
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Cursor**: Visible with click highlights

### Recommended Tools

- **Mac**: ScreenFlow or Camtasia
- **Windows**: Camtasia or OBS Studio
- **Microphone**: Any USB mic better than built-in (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB, etc.)

## Upload Process

After recording and editing:

1. **Export**: MP4, H.264, 1080p, 30fps
2. **Upload to YouTube**: Unlisted initially
3. **Copy YouTube URL**: Format `youtube:VIDEO_ID`
4. **Update module in admin**: `/admin` > Edit module > Add video URL
5. **Test**: View module page, verify video loads

## Tips for Better Videos

### Energy & Pacing
- **Start strong** - First 10 seconds hook the viewer
- **Vary your tone** - Don't be monotone
- **Pause strategically** - Give viewers time to process
- **End with action** - Clear next steps

### Visual Quality
- **Clean screen** - No clutter, close unnecessary apps
- **Zoom when needed** - Show small UI elements clearly
- **Use annotations** - Circle important areas (in editing)
- **Consistent branding** - Intro/outro slides

### Content Quality
- **Show, don't tell** - Demonstrate rather than explain
- **Real examples** - Use realistic agent scenarios
- **Common mistakes** - Show what NOT to do
- **Pro tips** - Include shortcuts and best practices
