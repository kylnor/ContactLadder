## Workflow #1: Logging Showings (1.5 minutes)

[**Screen: At a property showing**]

You just finished showing a property. Here's what to do:

**Quick Capture:**
1. Open Cloze app
2. Tap the microphone icon (bottom center)
3. Say: "Just showed 123 Main Street to the Johnsons. They loved the kitchen and master bedroom but concerned about the backyard size. They want to think about it and see two more properties this weekend."
4. Tap done

**What happens:**
- Cloze transcribes your note
- Attaches it to the property
- Links it to the clients
- Creates a timeline entry
- Reminds you to follow up tomorrow

Time: 30 seconds!

## Workflow #2: Creating Tasks On-The-Go (1 minute)

[**Screen: Inbox view**]

Client just texted you something important?

**From any screen:**
1. Long-press on the conversation
2. Tap "Create Task"
3. Or tap the conversation > three dots > "Create Task"
4. Choose due date
5. Done!

**Pro tip:**
Use the widget! Add Cloze widget to your home screen.
One tap to create a task.

[**Screen: Show home screen widget**]

## Workflow #3: Quick Email with Voice (1.5 minutes)

[**Screen: Driving or walking**]

Need to send a quick email but you're driving?

**Voice-to-Email:**
1. Tap compose (+ button)
2. Select recipient
3. Tap microphone in subject line
4. Say the subject
5. Tap microphone in email body
6. Speak your email naturally
7. Review and send

**Example:**
"Hey Sarah, just wanted to follow up from yesterday's showing. I know you were concerned about the HOA fees. I checked with the listing agent and they're actually lower than similar communities in the area. Plus, they include pool maintenance and landscaping. Want to submit an offer? Let me know!"

Tap send. Done!

## Workflow #4: Scanning Business Cards (1 minute)

[**Screen: At a networking event**]

Met someone new at an open house or event?

**Card Scanner:**
1. Tap People > Add Person
2. Tap "Scan Business Card"
3. Take photo of card
4. Cloze extracts:
   - Name
   - Phone
   - Email
   - Company
   - Title
5. Add tag: "Open House 1/15/25"
6. Save

Card is in your phone, contact is in Cloze, original card can be recycled!

---

## Workflow #5: Photo Documentation (1 minute)

[**Screen: Property inspection or issue**]

Need to document something?

**Quick Photo Upload:**
1. Open property or project
2. Tap camera icon
3. Take photo
4. Add caption (optional)
5. Save

Photos attach automatically to:
- The property
- Timeline
- Any related contacts

Later, they're searchable by property address or date!

---

## Workflow #6: Weekend Open House Mode (1.5 minutes)

[**Screen: Open house sign-in sheet**]

Running an open house? Here's your workflow:

**Before Open House:**
1. Create property (if not already created)
2. Create task: "Open House - [Address]"
3. Set checklist:
   - Sign-in sheet ready
   - Feedback forms
   - Property info packets

**During Open House:**
As visitors arrive:
1. Tap property
2. Tap "Add Activity"
3. Select "Meeting"
4. Select or create contact
5. Add quick note: "Interested in 3BR, budget 400-450k"
6. Done

**After Open House:**
1. Review all contacts who attended
2. Tap "Create Campaign"
3. Enroll in "Open House Follow-Up" sequence
4. Automated emails start tomorrow!

## Pro Mobile Tips (45 seconds)

**Tip #1: Home Screen Widgets**
Add Cloze widgets for:
- Quick task creation
- Today's agenda
- People to contact

**Tip #2: Siri Shortcuts**
"Hey Siri, add Cloze task" - Creates task via voice

**Tip #3: Airplane Mode for Focus**
Working on a big proposal? Airplane mode for 30 minutes. Cloze syncs when you're back online.

**Tip #4: Offline Mode**
Cloze works offline! Take notes, create tasks. Syncs automatically when connected.

**Tip #5: Dark Mode**
Settings > Appearance > Dark mode (saves battery at evening showings!)
