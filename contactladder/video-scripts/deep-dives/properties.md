# Properties Deep Dive - Video Script

**Duration: 15-18 minutes**

---

## Introduction (30 seconds)

Welcome to the Properties Deep Dive!

This is where Cloze becomes incredibly powerful for real estate agents. You're going to learn how to track listings, manage transactions, integrate with MLS, and never lose track of a deal again.

Let's get started!

---

## Understanding Properties in Cloze (2 minutes)

[**Screen: Properties tab overview**]

Properties in Cloze are different from regular projects. They're specifically designed for real estate with:
- MLS integration
- Transaction stages
- Commission tracking
- Client connections
- Document storage
- Timeline of all activity

Think of each property as a complete deal file that travels with you.

---

## Setting Up MLS Integration (3 minutes)

[**Screen: Settings > Integrations > MLS**]

First, let's connect your MLS.

**Step 1: Navigate to Integrations**
- Settings > Integrations
- Find your MLS provider
- Click "Connect"

**Step 2: Authorize Access**
- Enter your MLS credentials
- Grant Cloze permission to sync listings
- This is read-only - Cloze won't change your MLS data

**Step 3: Configure Sync Settings**
Choose what to sync:
- ✅ Your listings
- ✅ Your transactions
- ✅ Property details
- ✅ Photos

[**Screen: Sync in progress**]

Initial sync takes a few minutes. After that, updates happen automatically every hour.

---

## Creating a Property Manually (3 minutes)

[**Screen: Properties > New Property**]

Let's create a property record.

**Step 1: Click "New Property"**

**Step 2: Enter Basic Information**
- Address (Cloze auto-completes!)
- Type: Listing, Buyer transaction, or Just tracking
- Status: Active, Pending, Closed, etc.
- List Price
- MLS Number (if applicable)

**Step 3: Add Clients**
Link the buyers or sellers:
- Search for existing contact
- Or create new contact
- You can have multiple clients per property

**Step 4: Set Commission Details**
- Commission percentage
- Expected gross commission
- Your split
- Cloze calculates your net automatically!

**Step 5: Add Key Dates**
- List date
- Offer date
- Closing date
- Contingency deadlines

Save it - you're done!

## Automation with Properties (2 minutes)

[**Screen: Settings > Automation**]

Set up automatic actions when properties change status.

**Example: New Listing Goes Active**
Automatically:
- Send "Just Listed" email to your sphere
- Create task: "Schedule photos"
- Create task: "Update website"
- Add tag to clients: "Active Seller"

**Example: Property Goes Under Contract**
Automatically:
- Send congrats email to clients
- Create task sequence for transaction milestones
- Notify your TC
- Update your commission forecast

## Pro Tips (1 minute)

**Tip #1: Use Templates**
Create property templates with your standard stages, tasks, and automations. Speeds up new listings!

**Tip #2: Tag for Quick Filtering**
Tags like "Luxury", "Investment", "First-Time Buyer" help you filter and report.

**Tip #3: Weekly Pipeline Review**
Every Monday, review your properties board. Move stale ones, update statuses, create follow-ups.

**Tip #4: Client Satisfaction**
After closing, create a "Post-Closing" stage with tasks for reviews, referrals, and stay-in-touch plan.
