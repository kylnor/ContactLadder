## Relationship Scoring Explained (2 minutes)

[**Screen: Contact profile showing relationship score**]

See this number? That's your relationship score with this person.

**Cloze scores 1-100 based on:**
- Communication frequency
- Recency of last contact
- Response rates
- Meeting history
- Deal history
- Email engagement

**What the scores mean:**

**90-100: VIP Relationship**
- Frequent contact
- High engagement
- Recent activity
- These are your A-list clients and hot leads

**70-89: Strong Relationship**
- Regular contact
- Good engagement
- Active relationship

**50-69: Moderate Relationship**
- Some contact
- Relationship exists but could be stronger

**Below 50: Weak or Stale Relationship**
- Little contact
- Low engagement
- Needs attention!

**Here's the powerful part:**
[**Screen: Filter contacts by score**]

You can filter your entire database by score to find who needs attention!

## "People to Reach Out To" Feature (2 minutes)

[**Screen: Home > People to Reach Out To section**]

This is Cloze AI's killer feature.

Every morning, Cloze shows you exactly who to contact today.

**How it decides:**
- You haven't talked to them in [X] days
- But you USED to talk every [Y] days
- Their relationship score is dropping
- They're important to your business (past client, lead, referral source)

**Example:**
[**Screen: Show specific person**]

"Sarah Johnson - Last contact 45 days ago, usually contact every 30 days"

Translation: You're overdue! Call Sarah today.

**Taking Action:**
Click the person > Quick actions:
- Send email (AI suggests what to say!)
- Schedule call
- Add reminder
- Remove from list (if not relevant)

Takes 2 minutes to work through this list every morning. Prevents relationships from going cold!

## Common AI Mistakes to Avoid (1 minute)

**Mistake #1: Dismissing Everything**
If you constantly dismiss suggestions, AI stops learning. Give it a chance!

**Mistake #2: Not Setting Priorities**
AI doesn't know who's important unless you tell it. Use tags, scores, and preferences.

**Mistake #3: Ignoring Stale Relationship Alerts**
These are gold! Don't dismiss them. Act on them or adjust your follow-up cadence.

**Mistake #4: Not Connecting All Communication Channels**
AI needs complete data. Connect email, phone, calendar, and SMS.
