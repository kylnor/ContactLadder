## What is Cloze?

Cloze is an AI-powered relationship management platform designed specifically for professionals who need to stay connected with their network. For real estate agents, it combines:

- **Contact Management** - All your contacts in one place
- **Email & Communication** - Unified inbox for all your messages
- **Task & Project Management** - Keep deals and transactions organized
- **Automation** - Let AI handle the busy work
- **Properties** - MLS integration and transaction tracking
