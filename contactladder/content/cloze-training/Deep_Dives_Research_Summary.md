## DEEP DIVE 5: TEAMS

**Target Audience:** Team leads, admins, and teams who need to collaborate

**Key Features Found:**

### Four Sharing Methods

**1. Agent Team**
- Ongoing collaboration with multiple users
- Shares contacts, projects, deals, timeline activity
- Assign responsibility for clients/leads
- Contact info syncs across team
- Great for real estate teams, shared client management

**2. Deal (Project) Team**
- Time-bound collaboration on specific deals
- Shares project-specific contact info and timeline activity
- Duration-based (for length of deal, adjustable)
- Add coworkers as collaborators on properties/deals

**3. Inner Circle**
- Small, trusted team (usually 2 people max)
- Automatically shares emails, calls, texts, notes for shared contacts
- Contact records and tags stay private
- Works across all stages and segments
- No assignment needed

**4. Delegate Access**
- Grant another user access to work on your behalf
- Ideal for assistants, marketing support
- Control what delegate can see and do
- Cannot modify passwords or sensitive settings

### Sharing Controls
- Default sharing settings per Segment
- Automatic sharing for Business Segments (Customer, Partner, Seller, etc.)
- Manual sharing for individual contacts
- Bulk sharing for multiple contacts
- Shared Audiences for team-wide contact groups
- Sub-Teams for organization structure

### What Gets Shared
- Contact information (name, email, phone, address)
- Timeline activity (emails, calls, texts based on settings)
- Notes and At a Glance
- Tags (optional)
- Next Steps and reminders
- Deal/Project details
