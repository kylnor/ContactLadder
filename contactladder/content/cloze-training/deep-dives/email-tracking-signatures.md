**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### How Email Open Tracking Works

**The Technology:**
- Cloze embeds an invisible 1x1 pixel image in emails
- When recipient opens email, the pixel loads
- Cloze records the open event
- Shows you: date, time, and device used

**What You Can See:**

| Information | Example | What It Tells You |
|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| **If opened** | ‚úÖ Opened | They received and read it |
| **When opened** | Jan 15, 9:23 AM | Best time to follow up |
| **How many times** | Opened 5 times | High interest/engagement |
| **Device** | iPhone, Desktop | How they prefer to read email |
| **Location** (sometimes) | Las Vegas, NV | Confirms they're local/traveling |

**What You CAN'T See:**
- How long they read it
- If they read the whole email
- If they forwarded it (you'll see if forwardee opens it)

**Important Notes:**

‚ö†Ô∏è **Limitations:**
- Requires images enabled in email client (most do by default)
- Gmail pre-loads images = may show false opens
- Some privacy tools block tracking
- Not 100% accurate, but directionally reliable

‚úÖ **Privacy:**
- Cloze doesn't share that you're tracking
- Industry-standard practice
- No personal info collected beyond open event

**When Opens Tell You to Act:**

| Scenario | What It Means | Action |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| **Opened immediately** | Urgent interest | Follow up soon (within hours) |
| **Opened 3+ times** | Highly engaged, may have questions | Call to offer help |
| **Opened once, days ago** | Read but not ready | Gentle follow-up in a week |
| **Never opened** | Missed it, wrong email, not interested | Try different approach (text/call) |
| **Opened 10+ times** | Shared with others or analyzing deeply | Major interest - prioritize |

**Why this matters:** You've got 40 hours a week. Stop guessing who's interested. Let the data tell you where to focus your time!

## SECTION 2: LINK CLICK TRACKING

### Know What They're Interested In

**WHY (Video - 2-3 min)**

**Hook:** "You send 5 listings. They click 2 of them. Now you know which properties interest them. Without asking. Without guessing. The result: Laser-focused follow-up that actually converts!"

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### How Click Tracking Works

**The Technology:**
- Cloze wraps links in tracking URLs
- When recipient clicks, Cloze records the event
- Shows you: which link, when clicked, how many times

**What You Can Track:**

| Link Type | Example | What You Learn |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| **Property Listings** | MLS link, virtual tour | Which properties interest them |
| **Documents** | CMA PDF, market report | If they reviewed your materials |
| **Scheduling Links** | Calendly, showing times | When they're booking appointments |
| **Website Links** | Your bio page, blog post | What content engages them |

**Use Cases:**

**Buyer Emails:**
- Send 5 listings
- Track which ones they click
- Follow up on those specific properties

**Seller Emails:**
- Send CMA with 3 comparable sales links
- See which comps they reviewed
- Discuss those in next conversation

**Newsletter:**
- Include 3 blog posts
- Track which topics get most clicks
- Create more content on popular topics

**HOW (Video - 2-3 min)**

*Screen recording showing:*

### Part 1: Enable Click Tracking

1. **Settings ‚Üí Mail Settings**
2. **Find "Link Click Tracking"**
3. **Enable**
4. **Save**

### Part 2: See Link Clicks

1. **Open contact timeline**
2. **Find email with links**
3. **Click to expand**
4. **See click events:**
   - "Clicked: 123 Maple St listing"
   - "Jan 15 @ 2:30 PM"

### Part 3: Use Click Data for Follow-Up

**Example Workflow:**

1. **Send buyer 5 listings via email**
2. **Wait 24 hours**
3. **Check Cloze:**
   - Mike clicked 2 listings: 123 Maple St and 456 Oak Ave
   - Opened email 3 times
4. **Follow-up call:**
   - "Hi Mike, I saw you checked out the house on Maple Street. Want to schedule a showing?"

**Result:** Mike thinks you're psychic. You're just tracking engagement. Boom!

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "Do recipients know their clicks are being tracked?"**
- A: No. The links look normal. Tracking is invisible. (This is standard practice in email marketing and sales.)

**Q: "What if link tracking breaks the link?"**
- A: Rare, but if it happens, disable click tracking for that email or use untracked links.

**Q: "Can I see WHO clicked a link in a mail merge?"**
- A: Yes! Each recipient's clicks appear on their individual timeline.

**Q: "What if someone clicks a link multiple times?"**
- A: All clicks are recorded with timestamps. Multiple clicks = high interest.

## SECTION 4: AUTO-GREETINGS

### Personalize Based on Time of Day

**WHY (Video - 1-2 min)**

**Hook:** "Sending an email at 8am? 'Good morning!' Sending at 6pm? 'Good evening!' Auto-greetings do this for you. On autopilot!"

**WHAT (Text + Visual)**

### How Auto-Greetings Work

**Cloze can automatically insert:**
- Good morning (before noon)
- Good afternoon (noon - 5pm)
- Good evening (after 5pm)

**Example:**

**You write:**
```
{{Greeting}} Mike,

Just wanted to follow up on the showing yesterday...
```

**Recipient sees (sent at 9am):**
```
Good morning Mike,

Just wanted to follow up on the showing yesterday...
```

**Recipient sees (sent at 7pm):**
```
Good evening Mike,

Just wanted to follow up on the showing yesterday...
```

**HOW to Enable Auto-Greetings:**

1. **Settings ‚Üí Mail Settings**
2. **Find "Auto-Greetings"**
3. **Enable**
4. **Choose style:**
   - Formal: "Good morning"
   - Casual: "Hey" / "Hi"
   - Custom: Define your own
5. **Save**

**Using in Emails:**

1. **Compose email**
2. **Type:** `{{Greeting}}` or use autocomplete
3. **Cloze replaces with appropriate greeting**
4. **Send**

**When to Use:**

‚úÖ **Good for:**
- Follow-up emails
- Check-ins
- Professional correspondence
- Mass emails (mail merge)

‚ùå **Skip for:**
- Replies to ongoing thread (weird to say "Good morning" at 10pm in a reply)
- Extremely time-sensitive urgent emails
- International recipients (time zones)

**WHAT IF (Text)**

**Q: "What if I send an email at 11:59pm - will it say 'Good evening'?"**
- A: Yes. Might want to schedule send for morning instead if it's not urgent.

**Q: "Can I customize the greetings?"**
- A: Yes, in Mail Settings  define custom greetings or disable entirely.

**Q: "What if recipient is in different time zone?"**
- A: Greeting is based on YOUR send time, not their receipt time. For international contacts, skip auto-greetings.

### Email Tracking + Signatures Workflow

**Scenario:** Zillow lead comes in

4. **Monitor opens:**
